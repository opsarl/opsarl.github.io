INTRO

Solutions are our business

Founded in the Swiss pre-Alps in 2012 O'Brien & Partners are an independent IT & security consultancy made up of specialists who work in small teams.

Its roots date back to 1997, Redhat 4.1 and beyond and life has never been the same.

We usually get called upon as a response to a security incident or to gain external, objective advice and recommendations using our specialized expertise. In the past we have also been hired as temporary help during one-time projects or to manage the IT services you wish to outsource.

"Our mission at O'Brien & Partners is to get our clients educated, through any tough moments, assisting with our team's expertise and protect clients property and assets."

Dominic O'Brien - CEO
